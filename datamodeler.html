Data Modeler

We are currently seeking an Assistant Director to perform data analysis, profiling, modeling, source-to-target mapping and reporting for projects within our portfolio. In addition, the Assistant Director will participate in the development of innovative strategies to enable better data integration across multiple domains within the City.


  Responsibilities

Text goes here


  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Ability to work with internal and external stakeholders to understand their reporting needs, present them with various reporting options and guide them to the solution that best meets their needs.

Perform data analysis and profiling of legacy and strategic source systems to assess data quality and identify issues, inconsistencies and missing data.

Collaboration with ETL team and database administrators to implement the reporting platform design for business and program staff.

Develop, update and maintain the conceptual, logical and physical data models best suited for existing and future reporting needs.

Develop and maintain excellent documentation for reporting components such as data models, dictionaries, source-to-target mapping, ETL, reports.

Support business analysis and development of reports either via SQL queries and/or an enterprise reporting tool such as OBIEE.

Experience in modeling techniques and solutions in dimensional data models.

Analytical assistance and supervision, including quantitative and qualitative data analysis, the evaluation of best practices, and the identification and assessment of operational improvement opportunities at different agencies.


  Education and Experience

Master's degree in information systems, technology or equivalent preferred with 7-10 years experience in digital services and technology innovation.
