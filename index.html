• Mayor's Office of Digital Strategy

Help NYC bring digital design to everybody.

MODS is hiring a digital design coordinator to help build trustworthy public services by:

  • Creating design standards
  • Visual storytelling
  • Connecting NYC agencies with design best practices and community
  • Acting as NYC gov’s ambassador to the design community

Who we're looking for

The perfect candidate:

  • Has a passion for design for all, including accessibility, compatibility and multilingual content.
  • Digital design experience
  • User experience
  • Managing designers
  • Able to collaborate with civil servants, practitioners and developers
  • Can identify opportunities for projects and them into action

How to apply

If you think this might be you: apply!

Go to this page and put “[x]” in the job id field to find the official Digital Design Coordinator listing. Select “apply now”.
